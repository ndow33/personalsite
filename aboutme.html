Hi, I’m Nate -

A Solutions Architect who’s passionate about using technology to solve business problems. Some of my technical specialties include data wrangling/cleaning, ETL pipeline development, and implementing predictive models using libraries such as pandas, matplotlib, plotly, NumPy, scikit-learn, Keras, and TensorFlow.

Before becoming a solutions architect, I learned to speak Spanish fluently while serving the people of Argentina for two years as a full-time volunteer missionary. After that life-changing experience, I returned to the US to play two seasons of college basketball where I learned how to work hard, contribute to a team, take and apply direct feedback, and the importance of time management as I maintained a 4.0 GPA while earning an associate’s degree in general studies.

Shortly thereafter, I had the opportunity to start my career as an SDR at Go1, an Australian eLearning company. While working as an SDR helped me learn valuable lessons about sales, the importance of perpetual learning, the power of habit, and effective communication, I decided to increase my technical know-how by studying data science and machine learning at Lambda School in the evenings.

This combination of technical and sales experience helped me land a position as a Sales Engineer at Forethought where I learned even more about the sales process, how to apply AI solutions to real-world problems, and what it means to be a key player on a cross-functional team.

Currently, I work for BairesDev as a Solutions Architect and am excited to grow with them as I help our clients think through how they can best create solutions using our incredible pool of engineering talent.

If you’ve read this far, I’d love to connect with you and hear your story!
